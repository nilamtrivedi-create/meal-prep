LEMON TZATZIKI

Makes about 1½ cups

Ingredients

  • ½ cup finely grated Persian cucumbers (from about 2 Persian cucumbers)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, divided, plus more if needed ~5mL
  • 1 cup full-fat Greek yogurt ~232g
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil ~30mL
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated ~8g
  • Zest of 1 lemon (about 1 tablespoon)
  • Juice of ½ lemon (about 2 tablespoons)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint

Instructions

  1. In a small mixing bowl, stir together the grated cucumber and ½ teaspoon salt. Transfer the cucumber mixture to an absorbent paper towel, twist, and squeeze to remove the excess moisture.
  2. Transfer the cucumber back to the mixing bowl and add the remaining ½ teaspoon salt, Greek yogurt, olive oil, grated garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, dill, and mint. Mix until thoroughly combined. Taste and season with more salt, as needed. Notes: ● To store, transfer to an airtight container and keep in the refrigerator for later use.

Day 2

HERBED BEAN SALAD

Makes about 4 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar ~30mL
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated ~8g
  • Zest of 1 lemon (about 1 tablespoon)
  • Juice of ½ lemon (about 2 tablespoons)
  • ¼ cup chopped parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon chopped mint
  • ½ cup olive oil ~118mL
  • 1 (15-ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed ~425g
  • 1 (15-ounce) can butter beans, drained and rinsed ~425g
  • 1 large shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 cup thinly sliced sugar snap peas (3 to 4 ounces) ~200g
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more to taste ~5mL
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper ~2mL

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the vinegar, grated garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, and parsley. Slowly stream in the olive oil, whisking constantly, until the mixture is emulsified.
  2. Add the cannellini beans, butter beans, shallots, snap peas, salt, and pepper. Use a flexible spatula to toss to combine. Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or up to 1 day, to let the flavors meld. Taste and season with more salt and pepper before serving. Notes: ● To store, transfer the bean salad to an airtight container and refrigerate.

MEDITERRANEAN-INSPIRED MEATBALLS

Makes about 16 (1½ -inch) meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg ~57g
  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • ½ cup panko breadcrumbs ~30g
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated ~8g
  • 1½ teaspoons kosher salt ~8mL
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano ~5mL
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin ~5mL
  • ½ teaspoon ground sumac (optional) ~2mL
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper ~2mL
  • Zest of 1 lemon (about 1 tablespoon)
  • Juice of 1 lemon (about ¼ cup)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for cooking ~30mL

Instructions

  1. Whisk the egg in a large mixing bowl. Add the breadcrumbs, garlic, salt, oregano, cumin, sumac (optional), pepper, lemon zest, lemon juice, parsley, and dill, stirring to combine.
  2. Add the chicken and, using your hands or a rubber spatula, mix thoroughly until emulsified and slightly bouncy, about 1 minute.
  3. Heat ¼ cup of olive oil in a medium (10-inch) oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add the meatballs and cook, undisturbed, for 2 to 3 minutes, until a lightly golden brown crust forms. If the meatballs resist lifting from the pan, let them cook for 30 seconds longer — they will release naturally once properly browned. Flip and cook for 4 to 5 minutes more, until golden brown and the internal temperature reaches 165ºF. Note: ● To store, allow the meatballs to cool completely. Transfer to an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 4 days.

Day 4

PICKLED JALAPEÑOS AND SHALLOTS

Makes about 1 cup

Ingredients

  • 2 jalapeños, thinly sliced
  • 1 large shallot, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar ~59mL
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar ~50g
  • ½ cup water ~118mL
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt ~5mL

Instructions

  1. Place sliced jalapeños and shallots in a quart-sized deli container.
  2. Combine vinegar, sugar, water, and salt in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Once the liquid begins steaming, remove from the heat and pour directly over the jalapeños and shallots.
  3. Allow jalapeños and shallots to soften and come to room temperature before covering and storing in the refrigerator.
Day 5

MEDITERRANEAN BEAN SALAD

WITH MEATBALL SKEWERS
Makes 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 8 Mediterranean-Inspired Meatballs (page 8)
  • 2 (8-inch) wooden or metal skewers
  • 3 cups Herbed Bean Salad (page 7) ~480g
  • ¼ cup diced Persian cucumbers
  • ¼ cup red radish batons
  • 4 tablespoons Lemon Tzatziki (page 6)
  • ½ lemon, cut into wedges ~55g

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill or oven to 400ºF.
  2. Thread 4 Mediterranean Meatballs onto each skewer.
  3. Place the skewers directly on the grill, or on a baking sheet if cooking in the oven, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes until heated through.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the Herbed Bean Salad, cucumbers, and radish batons—mix thoroughly. Divide the bean salad between two large plates. To the side, on each plate, spread 2 tablespoons of Lemon Tzatziki, then top with a skewer. Garnish each with a lemon wedge for squeezing.
